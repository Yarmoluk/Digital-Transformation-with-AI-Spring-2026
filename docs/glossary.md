## Sample Terms

### Digital Transformation
The integration of digital technology into all areas of a business, fundamentally changing how organizations operate and deliver value to customers.

### Large Language Model (LLM)
A neural network trained on large amounts of text data to generate human-like text based on input prompts.

### Generative AI
Artificial intelligence systems capable of generating new content including text, images, audio, and code based on learned patterns.

### Prompt Engineering
The practice of designing and refining input prompts to optimize the output quality of large language models.

### Digital Maturity
The extent to which an organization has developed capabilities to adapt and evolve through digital technologies.
